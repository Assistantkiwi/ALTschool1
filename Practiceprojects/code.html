Compositon of Human Blood

The human body is filled with blood (which is made up of plasma, red blood cells, white blood cells and platelets.)
which is responsible for all the transmissions and movement of the body nutrients and excretions to and fro, around the body

Blood is comprised of two main components; Plasma and the Cellular components.
Plasma makes up 55% of total blood volume, while the cellular componenets form 45%

The cellular components of blood consist of a number of different blood cells or cell fragments, including Red blood cells, White blood cells, and Platelets.
